About Jain University

Jain University has been ranked as one of the best private universities in India by the NIRF. The Jain Online university which provides education entirely in online mode is also a well-reputed online institution. It is known for its premium and world-class online education.

The university has been approved by UGC-DEB to offer UG, PG, and Diploma level degree courses in online mode. The online MBA program of Jain Online University has been ranked as the best in Asia by the QS Global Ranking.

The university offers online UG, PG, Certificate, and Diploma courses in the domains of Management, Information Technology, Arts & Humanities, Commerce, and Media & Journalism. There are other short-term programs as well that are trending and in demand in the industry.

The university offers complete career assistance through various services and exclusive virtual job fairs in collaboration with their 300+ hiring partners. The university also has collaborations with Ivy League universities to offer global exposure to its online learners.

The university has its own LMS to conduct classes, and examinations, and offer other immersive learning facilities. There is a facility for personalized mentorship by not just professors but also top industry leaders who assist in the portfolio building of students with the help of real-world industry projects

Jain University Online Admission Process

The admission procedure for Jain Online generally takes place two times a year. The first admission cycle takes place between 1st Jan to 28th/29th Feb. The second admission cycle takes place between 1st July to 31st Aug. The whole admission process from registration to admission confirmation takes place in online mode. No entrance exam is reuqired for taking admission in the university.
