About Manipal University

The online Manipal university is a part of the prestigious Manipal University Jaipur (MUJ). The university started offering courses in online mode in the year 2021. The University Grants Commission (UGC) has entitled Online Manipal University to offer UG and PG programs.

The university currently offers BCA, BBA, and B.Com in the undergraduate category while MCA, MBA, M.Com, and MA JMC in the postgraduate category. These online courses are taught by the esteemed same faculty of the Manipal University Jaipur and real-world mentors.

The university offers an enhanced digital learning platform where you can find all your course material and cutting-edge tutorials. The university conducts examinations in online mode using this LMS. These online examinations are secure as they are remotely proctored. The LMS even provides self-learning opportunities to the students with the help of various practice tests and online quizzes.

The programs at the online Manipal are designed to offer comprehensive educational experiences to help students develop skill-sets that are relevant to jobs in diverse industries. The curriculum and the learning methodology have been designed not only by the professors but also by expert practitioners from relevant industries.

The university offers easy financing options like no-cost EMIs to help students from all backgrounds gain knowledge and education by overcoming the barrier of finances. There are also attractive scholarship offers for government employees, defense personnel, meritorious students, and differently-abled people.

The university has a sprawling campus with modern infrastructure and facilities such as libraries, laboratories, sports facilities, and student activity centers. It also has a strong alumni network and several collaborations with top universities around the world.

Manipal University places a strong emphasis on research and innovation and has several research centers and institutes devoted to various fields such as medicine, engineering, and social sciences.

Overall, Manipal University is a vibrant and dynamic institution that provides its students with an excellent academic experience and prepares them to be successful and responsible leaders in their chosen field.

Manipal University Online Admission Process

In online Manipal university the admission process takes place in online mode. Fresh admission starts from the month of January of every year. There are direct admissions, no entrance exam is conducted for the admission process. The admission procedure for 2024 for the online courses at Online Manipal University is as follows:

Step 1: Visit the official website of Online Manipal University and apply online by clicking on “Enrol Now”

Step 2: Upload the documents and then submit the application

Step 3: Pay the application fee. This fee is later adjusted in the Semester 1 fees.

Step 4: An admission counselor will reach out to you if any other procedure is pending and will assist you till the completion of the entire process.

List of Documents
  • Marksheets
  • Residential Proof
  • Proof of Date of Birth
  • Government Issued ID
  • Photocopy of Disability Certificate/ Employment certificate/ Service proof in case of Defense or Paramilitary forces (if applicable)
  • Undertaking by the applicant in case of any pendency (Result awaited/ Reappear/Backlog or any other pendency)
  • Undertaking for Gap Period (if applicable)
  • Two passport size colored photographs
  • Demand Draft (DD) for the prescribed fee

Why Online Manipal

Online Manipal University provides students with over 10 fully online, accredited degree and diploma courses.
