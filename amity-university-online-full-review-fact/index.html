Amity University has been ranked as one of the best private universities in India by the NIRF. The Amity Online university which provides education entirely in online mode is also a well-reputed online institution. It is known for its premium and world-class online education.

 

The university has been approved by UGC-DEB to offer UG, PG, and Diploma level degree courses in online mode. The online MBA program of Amity Online University has been ranked as the best in Asia by the QS Global Ranking.

 

The university offers online UG, PG, Certificate, and Diploma courses in the domains of Management, Information Technology, Arts & Humanities, Commerce, and Media & Journalism. There are other short-term programs as well that are trending and in demand in the industry.

 

The university offers complete career assistance through various services and exclusive virtual job fairs in collaboration with their 300+ hiring partners. The university also has collaborations with Ivy League universities to offer global exposure to its online learners.

 

The university has its own LMS to conduct classes, and examinations, and offer other immersive learning facilities. There is a facility for personalized mentorship by not just professors but also top industry leaders who assist in the portfolio building of students with the help of real-world industry projects.

 

Online Degree Acceptance

Online degrees earned from a government-approved university, just like a regular degree, are accepted by the top recruiters worldwide including government & private sectors.

 

Value of Online Degree

An online degree from a government-approved university holds similar recognition in the industry as that of a regular degree. Thus, it is equally valuable when considered for a job or higher education.

 

Quality of Education
The quality of education offered in online programs is of the same standard as offered in an on-campus program. Be it curriculum, faculty members, evaluation methods, practical sessions, online & on-campus programs follow similar standards of quality

Amity University Online Admission Process

 

The admission procedure for Amity Online generally takes place two times a year. The first admission cycle takes place between 1st Jan to 28th/29th Feb. The second admission cycle takes place between 1st July to 31st Aug. The whole admission process from registration to admission confirmation takes place in online mode. No entrance exam is reuqired for taking admission in the university.

The admission procedure for 2024 for the online courses at Amity Online University is as follows:

Step 1- Go to the official website of Amity Online (Note- there are separate websites for online and on-campus programs).

Step 2- Click on the “Apply Now” tab in the top right corner.

Step 3- First fill in your basic personal and academic details followed by your employment details (if any).

Step 4- Now upload all the necessary documents for eligibility proof along with your recent and coloured passport-size photo.

Step 5- Submit this application form and pay the application fee. Once your application is reviewed and documents verified, the university will inform you about your enrollment and further procedure via email.
